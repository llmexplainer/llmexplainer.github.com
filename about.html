About the project

How to Train Your Chatbot is an explainer about large language models (LLMs) and conversational agents (also known as AI chatbots), aiming to demystify the technology and build critical tech literacy amongst its wide range of users. It's part of a larger portfolio of games and explainers including The Algorithm and Survival of the Best Fit.
